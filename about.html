Short Bio

Based in Pokhara, surrounded by the Himalayas, I spend my free time riding bikes down mountain trails, swimming in pristine lakes, trekking hidden paths, and scouting unusual locations and characters that spark my creativity. I love cooking (my meals have been known to impress), and I’m always tinkering, questioning, and experimenting with new ideas.

Over the years, I’ve competed in and won robotics contests, tackled complex math challenges, and contributed to the children’s radio show “Milijuli Bolaun” funded by NGO Right4Children.

Films by Christopher Nolan and James Cameron, like Interstellar, Inception, and Avatar, fuel my imagination, while my work in visualization, simulation, and film allows me to merge science, art, and technology. Whether it’s directing a visually striking film, building a technical simulation, or solving a challenging problem—I’m always chasing the next big thrill.
